Folks, this is a hummus page

Yep, thats right, were talking about hummus on this page. That beautiful mix of chickpeas and tahini, probably some garlic, definitely some oil and likely some roasted red pepper that we all know and love. heat up your pitas, rip off a chunk, and scoop yourself up some of that delicious hummus goodness.

So, do you wanna make some hummus?

Well then, heres what you'll need

  • chickpeas, bout a can
  • two spoonfuls of tahini
  • couple cloves of garlic, maybe two
  • two tablespoons of oo (thats olive oil)
  • maybe some lemon juice, couple tablespoons
  • some good old salt and pepper

And then what do we do? Well, keep reading this page for starters

  1. get out your blender, and chop that garlic in it
  2. add the chickpeas to your garlic
  3. now add the lemon, tahini, and some salt
  4. blend her till shes creamy
  5. once smooth, transfer to your service bowl
  6. sprinkle pep on top, and drizzle with the oil
  7. serve! thats hummus buddy!
