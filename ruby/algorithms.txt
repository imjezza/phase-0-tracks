Elevator algorithm

- look at elevator door
- locate directional buttons adjacent to elevator door
	- determine if desired floor is higher or lower than current floor
		- if desired floor is higher, press up button
		- if desired floor is lower, press down button
		- if already at desired floor, turn around and leave
	- wait for door to open
- once door opens, enter elevator
	- turn around to face elevator door
	- wait for door to close
- once door is closed, locate and view floor buttons
	-  determine desired floor
		- if first button isn't desired floor, go to next button
		- if next button isn't desired floor, go to next button
- choose button of desired floor
	- wait for elevator to move to desired floor
- once elevator arrives, wait for doors to open
- once doors open, exit elevator
